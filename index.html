Hey there!

Annaliese Nurnberg is a visual journalist based in Columbia, Mo. She is currently a master's candidate at the Missouri School of Journalism, studying visual editing. Originally from Springfield, Mo., she loves meeting new and interesting people who congregate in Columbia.

In the summer of 2017, Annaliese worked as the photo editing intern at the Washington Post. She stayed at the Washington Post through December 2017, working on contract as a photo editor. Upon completion of her master's degree, Annaliese hopes to continue workging towards a career as a visual storyteller.
